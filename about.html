About Kioko Farms

Rooted in tradition. Growing with purpose.

Who We Are

Kioko Farms is a family-owned and community-driven agricultural initiative located in the heart of rural Kenya. Founded with the vision of merging sustainable farming practices with educational outreach, our farm serves as a model of environmental stewardship and community empowerment.

Through years of dedicated work, Kioko Farms has become more than just a food production site—it's a dynamic learning hub, a community center, and a demonstration ground for innovation in agriculture. We host students, youth groups, NGOs, and local farmers to exchange knowledge and best practices in crop cultivation, livestock management, agribusiness, and rural resilience.

Empowering Youth Through CBC Learning

At Kioko Farms, we embrace the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) as a transformative approach to education. We collaborate with local schools to offer real-life experiential learning opportunities that align with CBC principles. Learners visit the farm for hands-on sessions in environmental conservation, food production, and agricultural technology, allowing them to apply classroom knowledge in practical settings. From early learners to junior secondary students, we provide guided tours, farm-based assessments, and mentorship, nurturing curiosity and a love for agriculture from an early age.

Youth in Agriculture

We believe the future of agriculture lies in the hands of the youth. Kioko Farms actively engages young people through internship programs, volunteer opportunities, and agribusiness training. We work to shift perceptions of farming from a last resort to a smart, profitable, and noble career path. By providing access to land, tools, modern farming techniques, and entrepreneurial guidance, we empower youth to innovate and lead in the agricultural space. Our ‘Young Farmers Fellowship’ program is one of our flagship initiatives designed to mentor future farm leaders.

Kitchen Garden Setups

To promote food security and sustainable living, Kioko Farms trains households and schools on the creation and maintenance of kitchen gardens. Using recycled containers, vertical farming methods, and organic composting, we demonstrate how even the smallest spaces can be transformed into productive gardens. These setups are especially beneficial to peri-urban and rural homes looking to supplement their diets with fresh vegetables, herbs, and fruits. We provide seedlings, training kits, and regular follow-up to ensure every kitchen garden thrives.

Life on the Farm

Life at Kioko Farms is a harmonious blend of hard work, learning, and community. Each day begins with sunrise over the fields, followed by rounds to check on livestock, irrigate crops, and manage soil health. The farm operates as a community — involving neighbors, students, volunteers, and farmhands in a cooperative rhythm. From storytelling under trees to evening harvests, farm life here offers a unique balance between tradition and innovation. It's a place where sustainability isn't just a goal — it's a way of life.

Our Mission

To inspire and educate communities through sustainable agriculture, empowering the next generation with practical knowledge, food security, and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To become a leading model farm that integrates education, technology, and traditional farming methods to create resilient rural communities across Africa.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity – We operate transparently and with honesty in all our dealings.
  • Innovation – We embrace technology and modern methods to solve agricultural challenges.
  • Community – We believe in uplifting the society around us through inclusion and shared growth.
  • Sustainability – We care for the environment, practicing eco-friendly and resource-efficient farming.
  • Empowerment – We invest in youth, women, and underserved groups to transform agriculture into a dignified profession.
