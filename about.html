About Kioko Farms

Rooted in tradition. Growing with purpose.

Empowering Learners Through CBC Integration

Kioko Farms actively embraces Kenya’s Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) by offering practical, hands-on learning. We partner with schools to transform our farm into a real-life classroom where learners gain core competencies like critical thinking, creativity, and environmental awareness.

Youth in Agriculture: Cultivating the Future

Our Youth in Agriculture Initiative empowers young people to embrace agribusiness as a career. We challenge the outdated view that farming is low-skilled by introducing modern tools, agritech, and entrepreneurial strategies.

  • Fellowships and internships for university students
  • Weekend farming clubs for high schoolers
  • Seed funding for youth-led agri-projects
  • Digital marketing training for produce sales

Kitchen Garden Setups: Growing More with Less

Whether you're in Nairobi or Machakos, you can grow food even in tight urban spaces. We teach families, schools, and youth groups how to set up sack gardens, vertical towers, and bucket farms for daily nutrition and self-reliance.

  • Custom garden plans & layout designs
  • Free seedlings and organic compost
  • Workshops on composting & pest control
  • Monthly harvest check-ins

Life on the Farm: A Community in Motion

Life at Kioko Farms is a blend of work, learning, and laughter. From early irrigation routines to school visits and evening bonfires, every day is an opportunity to connect with the land and community. Volunteers and guests experience farm life through participation and shared stories.

Our Mission

To inspire and educate communities through sustainable agriculture, empowering the next generation with practical knowledge, food security, and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To become a leading model farm that integrates education, technology, and traditional farming methods to create resilient rural communities across Africa.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity – Honesty and transparency in all we do
  • Innovation – Embracing creativity and technology
  • Community – Uplifting each other through collaboration
  • Sustainability – Caring for the planet and the future
  • Empowerment – Equipping people to lead and thrive
