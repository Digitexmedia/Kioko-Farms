Welcome to Kioko Farms

CBC Learning

At Kioko Farms, we champion the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) by offering learners hands-on experience in agriculture and sustainability. Children engage in age-appropriate tasks such as planting, composting, and identifying plant species. We provide a practical learning environment where pupils connect textbook knowledge to real-world applications—helping them nurture responsibility, environmental awareness, and entrepreneurial skills from an early age.

Youth in Agriculture

We believe the future of farming lies in the hands of the youth. Kioko Farms engages young people through mentorship programs, school partnerships, and farm clubs. Youth are trained in smart agriculture techniques, agribusiness, climate-resilient farming, and technology in agriculture. Our initiative not only inspires the next generation but also equips them with tools to make farming a viable and modern career path.

Kitchen Garden Setups

To promote food security and healthy living, Kioko Farms offers guidance on setting up efficient kitchen gardens. From vertical gardens and sack farming to recycled container planting, we help households grow fresh vegetables in small spaces. We train families on composting, irrigation methods, crop rotation, and pest control—empowering them to produce nutritious food right from their homes.

Life in the Farm

Life at Kioko Farms is vibrant and deeply rooted in community and nature. Our day-to-day routines include animal care, crop management, and sustainable practices like rainwater harvesting and organic manure use. Visitors can explore the farm lifestyle, participate in communal planting, enjoy open-air cooking, and witness seasonal celebrations. It’s a place where nature, work, and culture coexist harmoniously.
